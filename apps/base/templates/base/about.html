About

Goobe's Subscription Box is a service of Goobe's Book Republic, an underground bookstore/library established in 2009 (Yes, we are the kind of fools that started a brick and mortar bookstore in the midst of an economic meltdown)

What makes us unique as a bookstore?
All of our staff are passonate about books and read atleast 3 books a month. We really love books, sometimes more than people. We have a serious thing for science fiction. Areas of specialisation are Non fiction, literature and Science fiction (not necessarily in that order)

What we really dream of?
More access to books, especially in areas where this is not possible. We run a non profit, called Go Book Bank, with the objective of setting up libraries and taking books further, sharing the joy and salvation that books have provided us.

Why should I sign up for this service?
Well, you benefit from our lifelong love for books and 8 years of being professional (ahem) book curators and sellers.
